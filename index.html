Grid-tied setups allow the end-user to maximize savings from day-time load usage, and utilize the Net Metering scheme.

Simpler Setup

Since grid-tied systems do not require batteries, they are easier to setup, monitor and maintain in optimal working conditions. No manual switching needed!

Reliable

The solar PV system simply supplements the power coming from the grid, which means that there are no power interruptions regardless of weather conditions!

Cost Effective

Appropriately-sized grid-tied solar PV systems guarantees maximum savings compared to other setups. Best return on investment!
